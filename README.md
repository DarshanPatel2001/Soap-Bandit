## 📌 Project Overview

The **BarSoapInfo.com / SoapStandle Knowledge Hub** is designed to become the most technically credible and consumer-accessible resource on bar soap.

The platform integrates:

- Ingredient science
- Sustainability data
- Public health research
- Retail and pricing intelligence
- Artisan and brand directory data
- Water chemistry and performance science
- Dermatological research
- Global hygiene impact data

### Long-Term Vision

- The **Wikipedia of Bar Soap**
- The **Consumer Reports of Bar Soap**
- A **Sustainability & Public Health Connector**
- A **Retail & Industry Intelligence Platform**

This project prioritizes **data credibility, scientific grounding, and structured datasets** rather than marketing content.
